# Larissa, Greece — Food Guide

Larissa sits in Thessaly, central Greece's agricultural heartland, so meat, dairy, and fresh produce are especially good here.

### Greek Salad (Horiatiki)

- Cucumber
- Feta
- Red onion
- Green pepper
- Kalamata olives
- Olive oil
- Oregano

### Tzatziki

- Cucumber

- Olive oil
- Dill

## Street Food

### Gyros
- Chicken / pork / lamb
- Pita bread
- Tomato
- Onion
- Tzatziki
- Fries (often inside)

### Souvlaki (skewers)
- Pork / chicken
- Optional pita, tomato, onion

### Bougatsa (popular breakfast in Thessaly)
- Phyllo pastry
- Semolina custard *or* cheese *or* minced meat

### Tiropita / Spanakopita
- Phyllo pastry
- Feta (tiropita) or spinach + feta (spanakopita)
